Website that called Trump impeachment 'a Jew coup' gets press passes for president's trip January 27, 2020, Michael Grynbaum, The Independent

To coordinate coverage of Donald Trump's trip to the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland, the White House provided press passes to the usual mix of American news organisations, including Fox News, Reuters and The New York Times.

One media outlet stood out: TruNews, a website aimed at conservative Christians whose founder, a pastor named Rick Wiles, recently described Mr Trump's impeachment as "a Jew coup" planned by "a Jewish cabal".
