Ben Garrison is an American political cartoonist. His cartoons have been widely promoted among the alt-right for various depictions of alt-right ideology. He has produced cartoons that showcase sexist, racist, anti-Muslim, antisemitic, anti-vaccine, climate change denial, and conspiracy theory content.
