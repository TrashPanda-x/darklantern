Secure America Now (SAN) is a politically conservative nonprofit group that focuses on United States foreign policy issues.

Funding

As a 501(c)(4) nonprofit organization, SAN is not required to disclose its donors. OpenSecrets reported that the main funders in 2016 were Hedge Fund manager Robert Mercer, former Best Buy CEO Brad Anderson, Best Buy founder Richard Schulze,Robert Maguire [https://www.opensecrets.org/news/2018/04/exclusive-robert-mercer-backed-a-secretive-group-that-worked-with-facebook-google-to-target-anti-muslim-ads-at-swing-voters/ Robert Mercer backed a secretive group that worked with Facebook, Google to target anti-Muslim ads at swing voters] Center of Responsive politics, April 5, 2018 and Estee Lauder heir Ronald Lauder, who gave $1.1 Million.[https://www.haaretz.com/us-news/ronald-lauder-gave-1-1-million-to-group-pushing-anti-muslim-campaign-1.5977658 U.S. Jewish Leader Ronald Lauder Gave $1.1 Million to Covert Group Pushing anti-Muslim Campaign], Apr 06, 2018, Haaretz
