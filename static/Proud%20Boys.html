Proud Boys is a far-right men's organizationPaige St. John & Veronica Rocha, [http://www.latimes.com/local/california/la-live-updates-berkeley-ann-coulter-conservative-and-alt-right-groups-1493319826-htmlstory.html Conservative and alt-right groups gather for 'free speech' rally in Berkeley], Los Angeles Times (April 27, 2017): "far right group Proud Boys..." with presences in the United States, Canada, Australia, and the United Kingdom. It was founded in 2016 by Vice Media co-founder and former commentator Gavin McInnes. McInnes describes the group as a "pro-Western fraternal organization" for men who "refuse to apologize for creating the modern world." Proud Boys has been described as both alt-right and alt-lite, Andrew Marantz, [http://www.newyorker.com/news/news-desk/the-alt-right-branding-war-has-torn-the-movement-in-two/ The Alt-Right Branding War Has Torn the Movement in Two], The New Yorker (July 6, 2017). but although McInnes has denied the group is alt-right is it one is is one of the few such groups still strongly active after a year in which the broader movement has been battered by lawsuits, deplatforming, and infighting.

The group takes its name from the showtune "[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lv0jav4lNsk Proud of Your Boy]," a song cut from the Disney film ''Aladdin'', in which the title character apologizes to his mother.Disser, Nicole (July 28, 2016).
