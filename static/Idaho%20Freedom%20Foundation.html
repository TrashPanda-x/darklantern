The Idaho Freedom Foundation (IFF) is a libertarian think tank located in Boise, Idaho. The organization's stated mission "is to implement innovative ideas to deplete the power of special interests and free people from government dependency." IFF is the parent organization of Idaho Reporter, an online news outlet covering Idaho politics. IFF is headed by Wayne Hoffman, a former newspaper reporter.
