Political activities

Shillman sits on boards of The Friends of the Israel Defense Forces, The Jewish Foundation for the Righteous, and the David Horowitz Freedom Center.

He also funds Brigitte Gabriel's ACT!, James O'Keefe's Project Veritas, FrontPage Magazine and Rebel News and argues against the US accepting Syrian Muslim refugees.[https://theintercept.com/2015/10/16/donor-refugee-backlash Strident Calls to Reject Syrian Refugees Fueled by Wealthy California Donor], Oct. 16 2015, The Intercept President George W. Bush appointed Shillman to serve on the Honorary Delegation to accompany him to Jerusalem for the celebration of the 60th anniversary of the State of Israel in May 2008.

In August 2018, it was revealed by The Times that Shillman had been providing finances to British far-right activist Tommy Robinson by helping to pay his "five figure salary", although at the time of writing this arrangement had ended.
