Trump phones Pam Bondi with Saturday night surprise: 'We'll be in Tampa very soon' May 20, 2018, William March, Tampa Bay Times

Hillsborough County Republicans got a surprise when President Donald Trump called in to their big annual fundraising event, the Lincoln Day Dinner, Saturday night.

For a few minutes, the crowd of hundreds listened raptly as Attorney General Pam Bondi, speaking to Trump on her cell phone, held it up to the mic at the podium.

Trump's message: "I'll be there fairly soon. We'll hold a special event there in the near future … We'll be in Tampa very soon."
