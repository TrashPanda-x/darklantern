FreedomWorks is a conservative and libertarian astroturfing group based in Washington D.C., and George Mason University. FreedomWorks trains volunteers, assists in campaigns, and encourages them to mobilize, interacting with both fellow citizens and their political representatives. It is widely associated with the Tea Party movement.[https://www.nytimes.com/2010/08/26/us/politics/26freedom.html Shaping Tea Party Passion Into Campaign Force], Kate Zernike, The New York Times, August 25, 2010
