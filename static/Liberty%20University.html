Liberty University (LU) is a private evangelical Christian university in Lynchburg, Virginia.

It is one of the largest Christian universities in the world and the largest private non-profit university in the United States, measured by student enrollment. , the university enrolls more than 15,000 students at its Lynchburg campus and more than 94,000 students in online courses for a total of about 110,000 in all.
