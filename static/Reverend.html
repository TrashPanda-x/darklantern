Reverend

Religious cloaking

In Scientology, the term "reverend" (as well as other borrowed Christian terms like minister, pastor, etc) is used by anyone who has completed the Scientology Ministers course. Until a few years ago, this course took roughly 15 hours to complete. It has been slightly extended with additional clay table demos.

For Scientology auditors, this allows them to claim ministerial exception and priest-penitent privilege over records such as PC folders.
